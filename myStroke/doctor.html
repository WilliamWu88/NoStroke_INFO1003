Dr David Steenblock

Dr David Steenblock and Personalized Regenerative Medicine's mission is to deliver advanced care for chronic and degenerative diseases such as stroke and traumatic brain injury, and offer hope to those patients for whom traditional medicine has failed to adequately serve. Over the last decade, hundreds of patients have been treated with stem cells as part of research-oriented clinical programs provided by physicians like Dr. Steenblock, as part of his practice of medicine here in the US (San Clemente, California.)
