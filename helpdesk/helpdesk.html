How can I login/register?

Login and register option is in the myStroke part which you can find at the top of any page. Press the button and it will lead you to a new page where you can login or register.
Or you can just click the link below
Register Login



I forgot my password, how can I reset it?

You can email us at chwu4078@uni.sydney.edu.au and we will send the password to you.



How can I change the style of the website?

You can change your style if it is not your preference. You can find the style button at the bottom of any page. Press it and you can feel free to change it.



What is the stroke test?

The stroke test link is at the top. You are required to answer some questions.
According to your answer, we will give you some suggestions so you can prevent stroke.
You can take test now! TAKE THE STROKE TEST



Can I share information of the website?

Of course you can. It is welcomed for you to share health knowledge with others so long as you don't use these information for commercial purposes.
