Who we are?

We are Information Technology students from University of Sydney. NoStroke.com is created in 2016.
The purpose of this website is to build healthier and wealthier lives, make people free of cardiovascular diseases and stroke.
No stroke is here to gather the general knowledge of cardiovascular diseases and hopefully this website can benefit all the public.
